Job Application Email Management App
Project Overview
Our company receives all job applications through one recruitment email account, which is currently managed by an administrator.
The purpose of this app is to help organise and manage job application emails more efficiently.
The app does not need to replace the email account. Instead, it should work with the existing inbox and help the administrator automatically organise emails, send standard replies, track responses, and manage the next action required.
Current Process
At the moment, the administrator handles the process manually:
Receive a job application by email.
Read the email and identify the position applied for.
Move the email into the relevant folder.
Send an initial acknowledgement email.
Wait for any additional information or reply from the applicant.
Track the applicant’s reply.
Send the application to the relevant team members for review.
Send one of several standard follow-up emails.
Move the email into another folder based on its latest status.
This process works, but it requires a lot of manual email management.
Proposed App
The app should connect to the recruitment email account and help manage the entire email workflow from one dashboard.
Main Features
1. Organise Incoming Applications
When a new application email is received, the app should:
Identify the applicant’s name
Identify the position applied for
Identify whether a resume or portfolio is attached
Categorise the application
Place or label the email under the correct job position or folder
Example folders:
Animation
Design
Illustration
Story and Writing
Production
AI Creative
Marketing and Content
Position Not Clear
2. Send an Automatic First Reply
After receiving the application, the app should automatically send a standard acknowledgement email.
For example:
Application received
Thank you for applying
We will review your application
We may contact you if additional information is required
The app should record that the first reply has already been sent.
3. Track Applicant Replies
If the applicant replies, the app should:
Match the reply to the original application
Keep the full email conversation together
Show that a new reply has been received
Update the application status
Notify the administrator that action may be required
4. Team Review
The administrator should be able to assign or share the application with the relevant team members for review.
The team should be able to:
View the applicant’s email
View the resume and portfolio
Add internal comments
Recommend the next action
Possible review decisions:
Proceed
Request more information
Keep for future consideration
Reject
Invite for interview
5. Standard Email Replies
The app should provide several prepared email templates.
Examples:
Request for portfolio
Request for additional information
Interview invitation
Application under review
Not selected
Keep in database for future opportunities
Follow-up email
The administrator can select a template, edit it if necessary, and send it directly from the app.
6. Email and Folder Status
After an email is sent, the system should update the applicant’s status and organise the email accordingly.
Suggested statuses:
New Application
First Reply Sent
Waiting for Applicant Reply
Applicant Replied
Waiting for Team Review
Additional Information Requested
Interview
Rejected
Keep for Future
Closed
7. Dashboard
The dashboard should clearly show:
New applications
Applications that have not received a first reply
Applicants waiting for a response
New replies received
Applications waiting for team review
Interviews
Rejected applications
Closed cases
Main Objective
The objective is to reduce the administrator’s manual email work.
The app should make sure that:
Every application is organised
Every applicant receives the correct first reply
Applicant replies are not missed
The team knows which applications need review
Standard replies can be sent quickly
Every email is placed under the correct status
